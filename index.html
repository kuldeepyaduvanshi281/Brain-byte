Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an assignment?
To submit an assignment, go to the Assignments page, find the assignment you want to submit, click the "Submit" button, and upload your file. You can also use the AI Assistant to get help with your assignment before submission.
How does the AI Assistant work?
The AI Assistant analyzes your academic data, assignment deadlines, and performance to provide personalized study recommendations, assignment help, and time management suggestions. You can also ask it specific questions about your coursework.
How do I contact my professors?
You can find professor contact information in the Faculty Directory. Each professor's phone number is listed, and you can use the contact buttons to call, save, or copy their contact information.
Can I sync my calendar with external apps?
Yes, you can export your academic calendar to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or other calendar apps. Go to My Calendar page and look for the export option in the settings.
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to Settings > Account Security > Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password and then set a new one. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
